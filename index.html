Extracting customer insights from Banking App reviews

Goal: Extract meaningful insights from customer reviews on Google Play Store of 6 banking apps in the UK, understanding customer sentiment and mapping main reasons for (dis)satisfaction.

Technical development:

  • Web scraping of +800k reviews from Google Play Store relative to 6 different banking Apps from UK
  • Text cleaning and preprocessing unsing Pandas and Scikit Learn
  • Sentiment analysis using VADER
  • Topic modeling using Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA)
  • Development of Streamlit web application to interact with data and gather insights

Business Value: Delivery of actionable insights to support product development and CX strategies.

Scouting football players

Goal: Scout and compare football players from a wide range of top football leagues, filtering the scouting criteria by position, skills, age and market value.

Technical development:

  • Web scraping +100 statistics from +9k players from +10 football leagues using data from Transfermarkt and FBRef
  • Text cleaning and preprocessing unsing Pandas and Scikit Learn
  • Feature engineering to develop aggregated skills composites
  • Development of algorithms to find top players for a given set of preferred skills that the user can select
  • Development of comparison charts using Ploty
  • Development of Streamlit web application to find and compare players

Business Value: Creation of a prototype that applies analytics to shortlist players, improving efficiency and accuracy in scouting decisions.
