About me

Hi, I’m Pedro!

After a few years of living the expat life in Madrid and São Paulo, I’ve recently returned to Portugal and now call the seaside town of Sesimbra home. Life here is lively — I’ve got two beautiful young daughters who make sure I never forget the meaning of “real-time problem solving.”

Academically, I took the management route — both my undergrad and master’s are in management by Nova SBE. Professionally, I’ve held several functions in the industries of financial services, logistics, and strategy consulting. Somewhere along the way, I realized my favorite part of every job was using data to make sense of messy problems, breaking them into smaller, solvable pieces.

I believe that in a world that’s changing so fast, curiosity, humility, and a love of learning are key to thriving. In that sense, last year, I decided to hit pause on my management career and fully lean into data: strengthening my skills with the latest tools, exploring AI, and building projects that bring these technologies into real-world contexts.

I also believe the best way to know someone’s work isn’t just by reading about it — it’s by seeing it in action. So, below you’ll find a couple of my recent projects, built in my spare time to keep pushing my boundaries in the fascinating world of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence. Hope you like it! Feel free to reach out if you want to chat about any of it.

Projects

Extracting customer insights from Banking App reviews

NLP · Topic Modeling · Streamlit
Python Pandas scikit-learn VADER LDA

Goal: Extract meaningful insights from Google Play reviews of 6 UK banking apps—understand sentiment and map top reasons for (dis)satisfaction.

  • Scraped 800k+ reviews from Google Play.
  • Text cleaning and preprocessing using Pandas and scikit-learn.
  • Sentiment analysis with VADER; topic modeling with LDA.
  • Built a Streamlit app to explore results and gather insights.
Web App GitHub

Scouting football players

EDA · Feature Engineering · Streamlit
Python Pandas Plotly Streamlit

Goal: Scout and compare players across top leagues, filtering by position, skills, age, and market value.

  • Scraped 100+ stats for 9k+ players from Transfermarkt & FBRef.
  • Cleaned data and engineered skill composites.
  • Algorithms to find top players given preferred skills.
  • Interactive comparisons with Plotly; Streamlit app UI.
